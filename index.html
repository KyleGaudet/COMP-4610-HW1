Kyle's 2022 Patriots Predictions

One of the best things about September each year is the start of the NFL season. After a long spring and summer without any football it's time to spend Sundays on the couch with family and friends watching games and eating some wings.


This year there have been a lot of changes to the Patriots during the offseason such as the signing of DeVante Parker and other players, the addition of the new draft class, N'keal Harry being traded (finally), and also Joe Judge, Matt Patricia, and Bill Belickick all being involved in the offense. Because of all these changes, as well as the changes to all the other teams around the league it will be interesting to see which teams are able to secure a spot in the playoffs and if the Patriots will be one of the lucky few that do.
